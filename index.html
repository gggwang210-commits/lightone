LIGHT ONE Healthcare AI Platform

Safety-first wellness AI for posture, low back pain risk reference, and CADSS. LIGHT ONE connects body-shape analysis concepts, risk-reference communication, and controlled AI decision support into one reviewable project flow.

Problem

Healthcare AI concepts need clear boundaries before they become user-facing products.

LIGHT ONE starts by defining what the system can responsibly support, where uncertainty must be shown, and when expert review should remain part of the workflow.

AI Over-reliance

Users may over-trust AI-generated feedback when limits, uncertainty, and review conditions are not visible.

Medical Misunderstanding

Wellness feedback can be misunderstood if language suggests professional judgment replacement.

Privacy, Bias, and Uncertainty

Body and health-related signals require careful handling of privacy, data bias, and model uncertainty.

Solution

A documentation-first path toward a safer wellness support platform.

The current MVP scope focuses on technical communication, safety policy, and a static landing page. It does not claim clinical validation or production AI deployment.

CADSS Workflow

Controlled AI Decision Support System

CADSS is the safety layer that controls how AI-assisted outputs are generated, checked, limited, and explained before they reach users.

1

User Input

Collect only the information required for the intended wellness support flow.

2

AI Analysis

Generate structured reference outputs with documented assumptions.

3

Risk & Uncertainty Check

Identify confidence limits, bias risks, and context gaps.

4

Safety Layer

Filter unsafe claims and keep outputs within wellness support boundaries.

5

Human Review

Route sensitive or uncertain cases toward expert review.

6

User-facing Explanation

Show clear, limited, and uncertainty-aware explanations.
