# LIGHT ONE Safety Policy

## Purpose

This document defines the safety boundaries for the LIGHT ONE project.

## Principles

- Transparency
- Human oversight
- Privacy protection
- Documentation-first development
- Responsible AI communication

## Safety Boundaries

- Outputs should be treated as reference information.
- Human review should remain available for sensitive contexts.
- Unverified performance claims should not be published.
- Regulatory status must not be assumed. [확인필요]

## Risk Areas

### Medical Misunderstanding
Users may misunderstand AI outputs.

### Data Bias
Training data may not represent all populations.

### Uncertainty
Model outputs may be incomplete or context-dependent.

### Privacy
Health-related information may require additional protection.

### Expert Review
Qualified review may be necessary before deployment.

### Regulatory Status
SaMD or medical-device applicability remains unresolved. [확인필요]
