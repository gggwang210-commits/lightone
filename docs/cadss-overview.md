# CADSS Overview

## What is CADSS?

CADSS (Controlled AI Decision Support System) is the safety framework proposed for LIGHT ONE.

Its purpose is not to increase automation. Its purpose is to control how AI outputs are generated, explained, reviewed, and limited before reaching users.

## Objectives

- Improve transparency
- Communicate uncertainty
- Encourage human review
- Reduce over-reliance on AI outputs
- Support documentation and auditability

## Proposed Workflow

1. User input
2. AI analysis
3. Risk and uncertainty assessment
4. CADSS safety review layer
5. Human review trigger when needed
6. User-facing explanation

## Risk Considerations

- Medical misunderstanding risk
- Data bias risk
- Prediction uncertainty
- Privacy and sensitive data concerns
- Need for expert review
- SaMD or medical-device applicability remains unresolved [확인필요]

## Current Status

CADSS is currently a conceptual framework and documentation effort. It is not a validated clinical system.
